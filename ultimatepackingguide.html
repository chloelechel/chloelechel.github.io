The Ultimate Packing Guide


I don't know about you, but I always seem to forget something when I'm traveling. When I was able to create a packing guide and use it, it was rare that I forgot anything! Since it's helped me so much, I wanted to share it with you! Without further ado, here is my ultimate packing list:

Clothing
  1. Tops (You might want to pack an extra just in case one gets dirty.)
  2. Bottoms
  3. Pajamas
  4. Jacket/Sweater
  5. Underwear/Bras
  6. Socks
  7. Swimsuit/Cover-Up
  8. Workout clothes and Tennis Shoes
  9. Shoes
  10. Accessories
  11. Laundry Bag

Toiletries
  1. Toothpaste/Toothbrush
  2. Floss
  3. Skincare products (Exfoliator, cleanser, toner, moisturizer, etc.)
  4. Shampoo/Conditioner (If you don't want to use the hotel's)
  5. Body wash
  6. Deodorant
  7. Razor
  8. Perfume

Technologies
  1. Laptop/Charger
  2. Tablet/Charger
  3. Phone/Charger
  4. Movies

Beauty
  1. Hair tools (Curling iron, straightener, etc.)
  2. Hair brush
  3. Hair products (Dry shampoo, hairspray, etc.)
  4. Makeup
  5. Makeup remover
  6. Makeup brushes
  7. Tweezers
  8. Nail clippers
  9. Nail polish/Polish remover

Carry-On
  1. Tablet/Phone
  2. Book/Magazine
  3. Snacks
  4. Water
  5. Journal/Pen
  6. Wallet
  7. Sunglasses
  8. Chapstick
  9. Inhaler (only if you own one! üòÑ)
  10. Compact
  11. Miniature sewing kit (In case one of your clothes rip)
  12. Retainer/Case (Only if you previously wore braces)

Packing Tips!
  1. Roll your clothing so that none of them get wrinkled! (It also saves space in your suitcase! üòè)
  2. Double-bag liquidated items such as moisturizer or nail polish so that if it spills in one bag, you'll have another bag to take it home to avoid spilling all over your clothes.
  3. Plan your outfits for however long your trip is so that you don't overpack!
  4. If you think or say while packing an item, "I might need this if...," don't pack it. Chances are, you won't need it.
  5. Always keep your valuables with you on your carry on just in case something happens to your suitcase.
  6. Pack hand sanitizer or baby wipes with you in case you get your hands dirty or you end up at a pit stop or airport that's really dirty.
  7. If you're traveling via plane, bring gum to help your ears return back to normal while landing.
  8. Fully charge electronic devices before you leave for your trip. Dead phones and computers are awful for traveling, and it can be dangerous to not have a charged phone.

I hope this assisted in your packing, and safe travels!
